Lanzhou University

updateTime：2018-06-02 09:34   hits：540    fontSize：T|T
Lanzhou University is a national key comprehensive university directly under the Ministry of Education. It is one of the key universities in the national "985 Project" and "211 Project". The school was founded in 1909. Its predecessor was the Gansu Law and Politics School established during the New Deal in the late Qing Dynasty. It was the symbol of the beginning of Gansu modern higher education and opened the precursor of Northwest higher education. It was expanded to Zhongshan University in Lanzhou in 1928, and was named the national Lanzhou University in 1945. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, Lanzhou University adhered to the Party's educational policy, adhered to the correct direction of running schools, developed and grew rapidly. In the adjustment of colleges and departments in Colleges and universities, it has been identified as one of the fourteen comprehensive universities in the country; under the guidance of the strategic objectives of "developing the northwest" and "establishing a strategic rear", it has become a university with an important strategic position in the national higher education structure, shouldering the responsibility of training senior specialized personnel and talents for the country, especially for the western region. The important task of developing science, technology and culture is to develop. Since the reform and opening up, the school has grasped the historical opportunity of the national strategy of "rejuvenating the country through science and education", "strengthening the country through talents" and "211 project" and "985 project". Taking emancipation of the mind as the guide and reform and opening up as the motive force, the school has improved its running level in an all-round way. In 2002 and 2004, the former Gansu Grassland Ecology Research Institute and Lanzhou Medical College have been established. The integration of the college into Lanzhou University has made the school's disciplines more complete and comprehensive advantages more prominent, ushered in a period of rapid development in history, and entered the ranks of first-class university construction with long academic history, strong school-running strength and outstanding school-running achievements.
